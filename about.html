Bio

Marco Bagnoli comes from a small town near Milan, Italy. He was 4 when his dad, a pianist and guitarist, first introduced him to music. During a fourteen-hour trip to the South of Italy, Marco's dad starts playing CDs upon CDs of Jazz and R&B/Soul artists like Stevie Wonder, Aretha Franklin, Pino Daniele and Giorgia. From then on, Marco is more than sure that his true passions are music and singing.

 Marco cultivates his love for Hip Hop music during his teens, when he attends a Hip Hop dance school. He also starts writing, producing and recording his own songs at the age of 14. Using random R&B and Hip Hop instrumental beats found online, Marco spends hours and hours in his bedroom, mastering his songwriting skills.

At 16, Marco starts taking singing lessons and it's not until he's 17, when he meets his partner, Marco Lastella, that he decides to take music very seriously. Together, they work on hundreds of songs, Marco L. producing and Marco B. writing melodies and lyrics.

In 2019, at the age of 20, he releases two 10-track LPs, "Within" (Feb. 15th) and "Know It All" (Nov. 15th).

Influenced by artists such as Rihanna, India.Arie, Ms. Lauryn Hill, Alicia Keys and many others, Marco takes both old school and modern Hip Hop drums, soulful pianos and synths, and conveys emotional yet energetic vocal melodies that at times remind of R&B/Soul ballads from the 90's.

His brutally honest and heartfelt lyrics touch topics like freedom of self-expression, balance of self-esteem and the courage to speak one’s truth at all costs.

He's currently working on his third project, "Neo", a 5-track EP inspired by "The Matrix" movie.

Everything, from the creation of the music, to the final masters, to the visuals of Marco Bagnoli's new project were produced entirely by him and Marco Lastella, in their little apartment, in Italy.
